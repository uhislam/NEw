Overview


Ramadan Referrals

  • Any campaign creator that creates a live campaign on LaunchGood is eligible to participate.
  • A referral is counted when a user successfully signs-up for automated giving as part of the Ramadan Challenge using the campaign creators unique team referral link.
  • A successful sign-up includes the user signing-up to schedule their giving for all of Ramadan and/or the last ten days of Ramadan.
  • Campaign creators will be awarded 100 USD between the time when Ramadan Referrals is launched via the Ramadan tab for campaign creators and March 23, 2023 (7pm ET) and 50 USD between March 23, 2023 (7pm ET) through April 8, 2023 (7pm ET) for every successful sign-up via team referral link.
  • Referral donation boosts will be reflected on the campaign by the end of Ramadan.
  • Ramadan Referral program ends on April 8, 2023 at 7pm ET.
  • One-time Referral Leaderboard contest ends on April 8, 2023 at 7pm ET. LaunchGood will review referral leaderboards standings before awarding prizes.

Ramadan Challenge Daily Contests

  • Any campaign live during Ramadan is eligible to compete & win.
  • Only donations from organic sources count towards the leaderboard. Any donations from LaunchGood features, including marketing packages, will be excluded.
  • Leaderboards will update every hour with final standings taken at 7pm EDT.
  • LaunchGood will review standings before awarding prizes.
  • Prizes will be awarded to top 10 campaigns on Most Raised and Most Supporters leaderboards daily.
  • If a campaign lands on both leaderboards, the campaign will be awarded the prize with the highest value only.
  • Campaigns can win multiple days up to 50,000 USD total from Daily Contests (Most Raised & Most Supporters) across the 30 days of Ramadan (March 22-April 21, 2023).
  • Note that the 50,000 USD prize cap does not apply to Ramadan 27. Campaigns who win 50,000 USD worth of prizes from the leaderboards are still allowed to compete and win additional prizes on Ramadan 27.
  • Prizes will be quadrupled on Ramadan 27 (April 17, 2023).

Contest Period


Ramadan Referrals

The Ramadan Referrals program begins when the Ramadan Referrals feature is made available for campaign creators via the Ramadan tab and extends until April 8, 2023 7pm ET. Only referrals acquired during this period will be eligible for the referral donation boosts per campaign.

How it Works


Ramadan Referrals

Overview

Campaign creators are eligible to earn donation boosts towards their campaign for every person they refer to the Ramadan Challenge.


How to enter

Any campaign that creates a unique Team Link from their Ramadan campaign tab is eligible to participate in the Ramadan Referrals program.


Rules

  1. A campaign creator must create their unique Team Link and save it within their Ramadan tab to participate.
  2. Team Links cannot be changed after they are saved.
  3. A referral is counted when a user successfully signs-up to schedule their ramadan giving as part of the Ramadan Challenge. A successful sign-up is when a user commits their daily giving amount, provides their payment information, and confirms their participation in the challenge. They can either choose to schedule their giving for all of Ramadan and/or the last ten days of Ramadan.
  4. Only unique referrals will be counted. If multiple family members sign up using the same credit card, only the first sign up will qualify for the donation boost.
  5. Successful referrals and donation boosts will be reflected on the campaign creators Ramadan tab.
  6. Campaign creators will receive an email every time a person joins using their Team Link.
  7. Referral donation boosts will be allocated to the campaign that the Team Link was created from.
  8. Donation boosts are awarded to individual campaigns - not a collection of campaigns or teams.
  9. If a campaign creator has multiple team members running multiple campaigns, they may all use the same Team Link. However, the donation boost will only be allocated to the campaign through which the Team Link was created.
  10. There is no minimum or maximum number of referrals.
  11. Top 5 teams with the highest number of referrals by the end of the Most Referred contest period will receive additional prizes. See below for prize amounts. Standings can be viewed on LaunchGood.com/Leaderboards .
  12. In the event of ties occurring amongst the top 5 Most Referred teams, the tied teams will split the total value of the boosts awarded for all the tied places.
  13. LaunchGood reserves the right to disqualify any team that is found to be gaming the contest. This includes but is not limited to activities that involve using fake emails, users signing up with credit cards that are not theirs, etc.

Donation Boosts:

  • Referral Boosts: 100 USD between Feb 22 and March 23, 2023 7pm ET and 50 USD between March 23, 2023 7pm ET through April 8th, 2023 7pm ET, for every successful sign-up via team referral link.
  • Most Referrals leaderboard one-time winnings for top ten most referred (standings will be taken on April 8th, 2023 7pm ET)
    • First place: 10,000 USD
    • Second place: 7,500 USD
    • Third place: 5,000 USD
    • Fourth place: 2,500 USD
    • Fifth place: 1,000 USD
  • Total = 26,000 USD


Boost Distribution

  • Prizes will be awarded to campaigns in the form of donations, or Ramadan boosts, from Ramadan Challenge members. These donations will be labelled as Ramadan Challenge in the donor report.
  • Actual Ramadan boost numbers can vary based on the number of donors signed up to give.
  • Prize distribution will start after a campaign wins a prize and may occur across several days. All prizes will be fully allocated by the end of Ramadan.
  • Campaigns who win 50,000 USD total in prizes from the daily leaderboards will be disqualified from winning any further daily contests. Note that 27 Ramadan (April 17, 2023) is exempt from this rule; all campaigns (within sponsors discretion) are allowed to participate in the daily contests on 27 Ramadan (April 17, 2023).


Daily Contest

How to enter

Any campaign that is live throughout any part of the contest period is automatically in consideration for the Ramadan Challenge Daily Contests.


Rules

  1. Ramadan Contest Period: 7:00pm ET March 22nd, 2023 through 7:00pm ET April 21st, 2023.
  2. Any campaign that receives any donations during the Ramadan Contest Period is eligible to win.
  3. Prizes will be awarded daily to top 10 campaigns in 2 categories (20 total):
    1. Most online donations per day (total raised online)
    2. Most online unique donors per day
  4. The contest will reset daily at 7:00pm EDT for both categories
  5. Campaigns can only win up to 50,000 USD total during the 30 days of the Ramadan Contest Period (excluding Ramadan 27)
  6. If a campaign lands on both leaderboards, only the prize with the highest value will be awarded. If a campaign is placed in both categories at the same place, the campaign will only receive one prize.
  7. Prizes are awarded to individual campaigns - no collection of campaigns or teams
  8. LaunchGood has the right to reject any campaign
  9. Only donations from organic sources count towards the leaderboard. Any donations from LaunchGood features, including marketing packages, will be excluded.
  10. If 2+ users are tied for a single place, Launchgood will split the total prize value equally among all users who have tied
